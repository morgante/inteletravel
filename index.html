About InteleTravel

At InteleTravel, we believe that there is no better travel guide than you. Drawing on data from your social media accounts, online shopping history, geo­location services and a short questionnaire, our app produces a personalized, digital travel companion to guide you through your next trip.

Easy Setup

You decide what accounts you would like to synchronize with InteleTravel. After that, just let us know when and where your next trip will be, answer the questionnaire, plug in your earphones and you’ll be on your way!

Location Aware

Relying on a network of satellites, your digital travel companion will help guide you around your new environment. Feeling lost is fun only when you want to.

Real People, Real Places

A strong network of experienced travellers, locals, and curators complements our to help us produce accurate itineraries that allow you to discover the heart of the places you visit.

Flexibility

As you go about your itinerary, you can adjust how much information do you want InteleTravel to provide you with: whether it’s a full day’s itinerary or just a few suggestions, you decide the parameters of your own experience.

Features

User Settings

InteleTravel can be adjusted as you go to provide the information you need. Let us know if our recommendations are useful by rating them.

Data­-Free

InteleTravel relies on a network of satellites to know where you are; no need to buy expensive roaming services, local data packages, or be constrained by Wi­Fi coverage.

Coordination

InteleTravel allows you to access various travel applications, from airbnb to TripAdvisor. Check-in on flights, book accommodations, and much more!

Calendar / Planner

Enable the function and InteleTravel will make dinner reservations and book tickets for shows you might be interested in watching!

My Places

Mark your favourite places and share them with your friends!

Media Player

InteleTravel curates site­-specific playlists and photo/video galleries you can enjoy as you go!

Check Yourself!

How much have you spent? How many steps have you walked? IteleTravel keeps track of this and much more.

Weather on-the-go

InteleTravel will let you know about upcoming weather conditions you need to be aware of.

And much more!

Every aspect of the travel experience is customized for your delight.
